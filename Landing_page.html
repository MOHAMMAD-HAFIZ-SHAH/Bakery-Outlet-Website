Our Story

A Valley's
most trusted
taste

Born in the heart of Ganderbal, Shah Bakery & Fastfood was founded on a simple belief: that food made with the right ingredients, the right care, and a little extra love will always find its way home.

From our traditional Kashmiri baked goods to sizzling fast food, every item on our menu is a tribute to the rich culinary heritage of the valley — freshened daily, never compromised.
